# HR Zone Reference Tables

## Zones by Max HR %

| Zone | Name | % Max HR | RPE (1–10) | Feel |
|------|------|----------|------------|------|
| Z1 | Recovery | 50–60% | 2–3 | Very easy, conversational |
| Z2 | Aerobic Base | 60–70% | 4–5 | Comfortable, can speak full sentences |
| Z3 | Tempo (Grey Zone) | 70–80% | 6–7 | Moderately hard, short phrases only |
| Z4 | Threshold | 80–90% | 7–8 | Hard, 1–2 words, lactate threshold |
| Z5 | VO2 Max | 90–100% | 9–10 | Maximum, unsustainable beyond ~6 min |

## Zones by LTHR % (Lactate Threshold HR)

| Zone | % LTHR |
|------|--------|
| Z1 | <81% |
| Z2 | 81–89% |
| Z3 | 90–93% |
| Z4 | 94–99% |
| Z5 | >100% |

## How to Estimate LTHR
Average HR from the final 20 minutes of a flat, all-out 30-minute time trial effort.

## How to Estimate Max HR
Simple: 220 − age (rough estimate only)
Better: Recent all-out 5K race or hill repeat — peak HR observed is close to max HR.
